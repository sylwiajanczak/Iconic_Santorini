Santorini Luxury Hotel in Imerovigli

A timeless experience awaits guests of the Iconic Santorini, our distinctive boutique cave hotel is located on the beautiful island of Santorini, in the charming and picturesque village of Imerovigli. The spectacular hillside setting is literally carved from the volcanic wall of the caldera, our tranquil retreat offers breathtaking views of the azure waters of the Aegean Sea from all five levels of our Santorini luxury hotel.

All the rooms and suites of our lovely boutique hotel are unique, featuring personal veranda seating high above the wonders of this ancient volcanic island. A formidable addition to the list of Santorini luxury hotels, a stay at our delightful boutique cave hotel ensures unforgettable memories of your stay in Greece.

A unique Santorini luxury hotel combining tranquility, warm Hellenic hospitality, and luxury…

Guests of Iconic Santorini, a boutique cave hotel enjoy a refined atmosphere amid tranquil residential surroundings with each custom-tailored visit. Our dedicated staff are committed to providing the most exceptional service to our valued guests, where traditional Hellenic hospitality ensures each guest is treated like family, welcomed as a special visitor to our island home and cared for individually.

From authentic Greek dinners to personalized excursions, each guest receives unrivalled service and personal care. The first class service and luxury amenities combine with unparalleled serenity and magnificent views, creating truly unforgettable and magical moments to cherish.
