So, what's the STORY?

A timeless experience awaits visiting friends of Iconic Santorini, an authentic boutique cave hotel set in picturesque and romantic Imerovigli village. Situated on a spectacular hillside setting and literally carved from the historic volcanic caldera wall, our haven of tranquility on Santorini in the Cyclades islands was named Greece’s Leading Boutique Hotel for 2014, 2015, 2016 and 2017 at the prestigious World Travel Awards.
