The Iconic Suite

Our most lavish accommodation, The Iconic Suite captures the true class and grace of Santorini, icon of the Greek isles. The generous sized living area, elegant bedroom and quixotic indoor jetted grotto pool open to a generous dedicated terrace, with an outdoor jetted pool and dramatic views overlooking the azure blue waters below.

These elements elevate our most spacious residential refuge to new heights of exceptional style, intimate luxury and unparalleled sophistication. Splendorous design features set against local stonework and the original oven of this authentic cave home come together honouring the history of this iconic island. A stay in The Iconic Suite warms your heart and soul, forever finding its place among your fondest memories.
