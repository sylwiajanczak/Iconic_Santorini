The Hotel

At Iconic Santorini, a boutique cave hotel, true Hellenic hospitality and a warm welcome awaits valued guests, who can expect sincere personalized service within a relaxed residential atmosphere. World-class hospitality, a stunning authentic design and the highest quality amenities come together in our stunning Santorini luxury boutique hotel, with its breathtaking location, affording mesmerizing views of the and the glistening blue waters of the Aegean Sea.

Be it relaxing in the crystal waters of the dreamy outdoor infinity pool, sunbathing on the caldera sun terrace, sampling the local delights in in our authentic and home-style restaurant, or spending time in a luxury Greek-style residence, valued guests enjoy a sublime experience at our Santorini luxury boutique hotel. There is no feeling of overcrowding and the tranquil facilities at Iconic Santorini all contribute to stress free days of rest and a truly timeless experience.

Facilities at the Iconic Santorini luxury boutique hotel include:

Authentic Home-Style Restaurant (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Private Dining Areas

Terrace Seating / Lounge Area

Spa Providing Single or Couples Massage with Indoor Grotto Pool

Complimentary Wireless High-Speed Internet Access Throughout Property

Multilingual Staff

Major Credit Cards Accepted (Amex, Diners, Master, Visa)

Complimentary Use of Gym with Professional Quality Exercise Equipment

Outdoor Infinity Pool with Jetted Tub Seating
